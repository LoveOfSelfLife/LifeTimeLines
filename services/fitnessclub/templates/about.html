Active Friends Fitness

About Our Club

Active Friends Fitness is a private club located in and around Cranford, New Jersey. Our mission is to provide the tools and structure to encourage and motivate members toward long-lasting fitness while offering a cost-effective alternative to private gym memberships.

What We Offer

Through the ActiveFriendsClub.com platform, members can:

  • Join fitness events created by other members or create their own events.
  • Opt-in to receive SMS and email notifications about upcoming events and participants.
  • Access a library of exercise programs tailored for flexibility, balance, endurance, and strength.
  • Track personal fitness progress over time to visualize improvements and stay motivated.

Who Should Join?

This club is designed for experienced fitness enthusiasts who already have knowledge of proper exercise techniques. Members are encouraged to support and assist each other, but each individual is ultimately responsible for their own safety.

Why Join?

Exercising is more enjoyable and motivating when done with friends. Active Friends Fitness makes it easy to coordinate gym visits with fellow members, offering a social and structured approach to fitness without the high costs of traditional gyms.
