Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

At Active Friends Fitness Club, we respect your right to privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly. This Data Deletion Policy outlines how members can request the deletion of their personal information and how we process those requests.

What Data We Store

We collect and store the following personal information from our members:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Phone Number

This data is used exclusively for club-related communication purposes.

Your Right to Deletion

You have the right to request that your personal information be deleted from our systems at any time. Upon such a request, we will take reasonable steps to permanently delete your information from our active databases and any backup systems, unless we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, please send an email to:

Email: admin@activefriendsclub.com

Include your full name and the email address or phone number associated with your membership so we can verify your identity and process your request.

Processing Timeline

We will acknowledge your request within 7 days to 14 days, barring any legal or technical constraints.

Limitations

Please note that deleting your personal data will revoke your access to any member-specific features or communications provided through our site. Your membership may be considered terminated once deletion is complete.
